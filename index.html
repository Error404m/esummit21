E-Summit'21

A Techno-Industrial Colloquium

IIITA Info Communication and Incubation Center aka IIIC, is a business incubator set up under the aegis of IIIT Allahabad catalyzed and supported by the MEITY(Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology) under the Startup India Project of Govt. of India. IIIC is associated with NewGen IEDC, DST (Department of Science and Technology), NSTEDB (National Science & Technology Entrepreneurship Development Board), EDII (Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India), Startup India, and MeitY (Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology). IIIC is organizing its annual flagship event E-Summit 2021: A Techno-Industrial Colloquium a 3-day Techno-Business extravaganza from 25th to 27th March 2021. We believe that entrepreneurship is the key that can unlock India's latent inventive potential. It will not only push India to the forefront of the world but also help raise the quality of life in the country. . We heartily invite everyone to participate in E-Summit'21 which will be structured completely differently from previous editions and organized in virtual mode on Townscript Platform :

Day-0 : FinTech and Emerging Startups (25th March 2021):
People from Investment Banks, Hedge Funds, Blockchain Technologists, Emerging Entrepreneurs, Angel Investors, Venture Capitalists and Leading Quant people are on the radar.

Day-1 : Different Aspects of AI and Robotics (26th March 2021):
Prominent speakers from most advanced organizations, universities and the IEEE society seeking the involvement of IIITA's Faculty and industrial collaborations. Panel Discussion, keynote talks and workshops are likely to take place on this day. Organizations like Nvidia, OpenAI, FastAI, HuggingFace, DeepMind, Facebook AI, Google Brain, Stanford Group, Landing.AI, Tesla, SpaceX, CERN, AWS, IBM etc. are some on the radar.

Day-2 : Green Energy and Mindfulness (27th March 2021): After the 2 fully power packed days of BizTech wisdom, we will dedicate our this day to entertainment lite, sustainable energy advancements, and holding some powerful mindfulness sessions by renowned personalities around the world to hopefully rejoice our minds. We plan to conduct workshops from Mindvalley (Vishen Lakhiani) and Isha Foundation (Sadhguru) and also by Gurudev Sri Sri from Art of Living.
